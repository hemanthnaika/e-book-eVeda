Who We Are

eVeda started with a vision to bring the joy of reading to readers around the globe. We believe that knowledge and stories should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our curated collection includes eBooks from diverse genres, from fiction and self-help to academic resources, catering to all kinds of readers.

Our Story

Founded in 2002, eVeda was born from a passion for literature and technology. What started as a small collection of eBooks has now become a vibrant digital library serving thousands of readers.

We are committed to creating a seamless reading experience, empowering readers with easy access to books in digital formats, and fostering a community of book lovers.
